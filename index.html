About Me

A Complicated CV — Born a Long Time Ago

Environmental professional with a unique journey from business leadership to nature conservation. I have combined decades of experience in sales, HR, and management with current expertise in environmental protection and ecological education.

Specialized in bridging the gap between the digital world and natural authenticity through experiential education, I work with remote mountain communities in South-West Romania — protecting nature while preserving culture.

Fluent in Romanian, English, German, and French, with extensive experience in international business and diverse cultural settings. Over 8 years managing protected areas and working with remote communities, specializing in anthropology and cultural preservation.

"From decades in diverse business to recycling and now meaningful environmental work, I am passionate about bridging the gap between digital world and natural authenticity. My mission is to inspire others through experiential education, sharing the profound unique connection between human life and the Life which is more than human: the Nature."

— Gheorghe Lungu
